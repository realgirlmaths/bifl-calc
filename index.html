How this works: The breakeven price is the present value of all future cheap purchases, where each year's cost grows with inflation and is discounted at your return rate (growing annuity PV). This is the most you could rationally pay for the quality item. The cumulative cheap cost line shows actual nominal spend including inflation each year.
